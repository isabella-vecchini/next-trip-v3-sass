All our app features

Import and view all your tickets and reservations

Say goodbye to scattered emails, screenshots, and paper tickets. Our app gathers all your travel information—flights, accommodations, transportation, and event bookings—into one streamlined timeline. With instant access to your plans, you'll always know where you're headed next, stress-free.

Create travel itineraries

Organize your trips with ease using our intuitive itinerary builder. Simply drag and drop activities, set times, and add notes to design the ultimate travel schedule tailored to your style. Whether it’s a chill day of sightseeing or a packed itinerary, we’ll help you plan it all in a breeze.

Share it and collaborate

Planning trips with your crew has never been easier. Share your itineraries, suggest changes, and vote on activities—all within the app. Keep everyone in the loop and ensure the entire group is ready for an unforgettable adventure together.

Track and split expenses

Traveling on a budget? Our app helps you stay on track by letting you log and categorize expenses for every trip. From transportation to meals and activities, easily monitor your spending and split costs with friends for a hassle-free experience.

Post and read reviews

Discover hidden gems and avoid tourist traps! Share your experiences by posting reviews of places you've visited or browse recommendations from other young travelers like you.

Explore suggestions

Get personalized recommendations for must-visit spots, hidden gems, and unique experiences tailored to your preferences. Whether you're into hiking trails, cozy cafes, or vibrant nightlife, our app helps you explore like a local.
