What is Next Trip?

Next Trip is a travel and itinerary management app designed to simplify the lives of adventurous travelers. Whether you're backpacking across Europe, planning a weekend getaway, or juggling group trips, Next Trip makes it easy to organize your plans, collaborate with friends, and stay on track while exploring the world. Our story

I'm Isabella and Next Trip is a personal project I'm developing as a UX/UI designer with a passion for travel and technology. Based in Barcelona, I'm combining my skills in user experience design and web development to create an app that reflects the needs of modern travelers. This project is just the beginning, and I'm excited to see how it evolves as I grow as a designer and developer!

Our mission and vision

Our mission is to empower travelers by providing tools that make trip planning effortless, exciting, and collaborative. We envision a future where travel is more accessible and stress-free, allowing adventurers to focus on creating unforgettable memories rather than worrying about logistics.

Our values

  • User-Centric Design: We prioritize the needs of travelers, ensuring our app is intuitive and enjoyable to use.
  • Sustainability: We believe in supporting mindful travel that respects local cultures and the environment.
  • Community: Werre dedicated to fostering connections, whether it's among travelers, local businesses, or our growing team.
  • We embrace creativity and technological advancement to continually improve the travel experience.
